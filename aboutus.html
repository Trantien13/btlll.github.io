OUR STORY___

As a start-up established by enthusiastic and passionate young people with the love for drinks, students of UTC held their opening ceremony and launched Coffee Corner with the brand 10.Net Coffee at workshop 03 Cau Giay, Lang Thuong, Ha Noi on October 24th, 2022. Starting from their limitless hobby and passion for coffee, after learning and starting business with the project Cafe with love Store, the team luckily got the cooperative investment, and then 10.Net was born. The first showroom of 10.Net is located at No. 03 Cau Giay, Lang Thuong, District Dong Da,Ha Noi. This is a small showroom with tropical-theme design. Customers may enjoy cups of coffee right there or take away, or simply visit the coffee shop to learn more about coffee and share their passion with the team of passionate young people there. In addition, with the opening of 10.Net Corner at UTC , workers and staffs working at class CNTT1 UTC K62 have one more trusted place to enjoy coffee, chat and relax every morning or in the break. With the objective to make “your personalized coffee”, 10.Net will surely bring a new breath for Vietnamese coffee market. We believes that, with passion and constant learning of the team, 10.Net is promised to develop more, creating better kinds of coffee with high quality and becoming a famous coffee brand in Vietnam.
